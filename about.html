Our Mission

Campus Tools & Tips is dedicated to empowering Nigerian university students with easy-to-use online tools, practical study tips, and curated academic resources tailored to the unique challenges of Nigerian tertiary education.

We aim to simplify your academic journey, helping you manage your time effectively, prepare well for exams, and access the right materials that boost your performance.

Why We Created This Site

Navigating university life in Nigeria can be challenging due to irregular schedules, limited access to quality materials, and lack of effective study planning. This site was created to fill those gaps by offering:

  • Simple, accurate tools like our CGPA calculator that reflect Nigerian university grading systems.
  • Practical study tips based on proven strategies and real student experiences.
  • Free downloadable planners and checklists to help you stay organized.
  • Guidance on where to legally find past questions, textbooks, and other academic resources.

About the Creator

Campus Tools & Tips is developed and maintained by Samson Ugboke, a Nigerian student and tech enthusiast passionate about education technology. Samson understands the struggles Nigerian students face and uses his skills to build practical solutions that help students succeed.
