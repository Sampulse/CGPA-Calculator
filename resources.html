Departmental Library & School Portal

Most departments keep past questions and thesis/project reports in their library or on the departmental portal. Ask your departmental librarian or check notice boards and official pages.

University e-Learning / LMS

Many Nigerian universities (including NOUN, UNILAG, UNN, ABU) host course materials on an LMS. Always check your faculty or course page for lecture notes and sample tests.

Seniors & Student Associations

Soon-to-graduate students often have past questions and notes. Join your departmental forum or WhatsApp groups, but verify sources and avoid sharing copyrighted files without permission.

How to Use Past Questions Effectively

  1. Collect widely: Get past questions for at least the last 5 years if possible.
  2. Organize by topic: Group questions by unit/topic so you can practice specific weaknesses.
  3. Practice under exam conditions: Time yourself and mark strictly.
  4. Analyze mistakes: Create an error log and revisit those topics in your notes.
  5. Discuss with peers: Use study groups to compare solutions and different approaches.
