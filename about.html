Information We Collect

Campus Tools & Tips does not collect personal data such as your name, email, or location when you visit the site. We do not require user accounts or registrations.

The only data collected may come from Google AdSense or Google Analytics scripts embedded on the site to show relevant ads and monitor site usage. This data is managed by Google according to their privacy policies.

Use of Cookies

We do not place cookies on your device ourselves. However, third-party services like Google AdSense may use cookies to personalize ads and track user interactions. You can manage cookies through your browser settings.

Data Sharing & Security

We do not share your personal information with any third parties. All data collected by third-party advertising or analytics services is subject to their own privacy terms.

Our website uses HTTPS to encrypt data transmitted between your browser and our server for your safety.

Your Choices

You can choose to disable third-party cookies in your browser or use ad blockers if you prefer not to see personalized ads.

If you have questions about your data or privacy on this site, please contact us at support@sampulse.github.io.
