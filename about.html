Our Mission

Campus Tools & Tips is dedicated to empowering Nigerian university students to achieve academic success through accessible, easy-to-use online tools, effective study strategies, and carefully curated educational resources.

We understand the unique challenges students face in Nigerian tertiary institutions, from managing irregular academic calendars to accessing quality study materials, and we strive to bridge these gaps.

What We Offer

  • CGPA Calculator: An accurate, user-friendly tool tailored to Nigerian university grading systems.
  • Study Tips: Proven strategies to enhance learning efficiency and exam preparedness.
  • Resources: Guides on obtaining past questions, recommended study materials, and downloadable planners to help you organize your academic life.

About the Creator

Campus Tools & Tips was created by Samson Ugboke, a passionate Nigerian student and developer focused on education technology. Samson builds practical solutions aimed at helping students overcome academic hurdles and succeed in their studies.

If you have suggestions, ideas, or want to contribute, feel free to reach out.
